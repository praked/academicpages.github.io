Ongoing Projects & Interests

I'm currently working on my thesis where, broadly, my goal is to develop an scalable distributed agent environment for swarm robotics experiments. The platform is a low-cost experimentation platform that can be used for any available swarm robot with minimal or no changes. The said platform helps the user to collect state data from the individual robots during the experiments. The platform provides the user with a glimpse of both the micro and macro world of swarm robots during a test. I am working on supporting heterogeneous agents onto the said platform. The platform takes inspiration from Cellular Automata with a collection of agents and patches located in parallel, one on top of the other. The platform also exploits the phenomenon of Stigmergy, which is a mechanism of indirect coordination, through the environment, between agents or actions.

I am also interested in multi-agent robotics and its applications, human-robot interaction. Building novel robots has always excited me.

Apart from my work, I enjoy travelling and learning about new cultures and languages. I really like to find interconnections and variation of languages of different places. I love to sketch and paint. I am also an avid reader of world politics and history.
